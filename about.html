What is this tool?

Are you Date'n a Felon?! is a simple tool that allows you to search the jails and federal databases to check if your online date is a potential criminal.

How it works?

Just enter the information about your potentail date to see if they have been arrested or are currently wanted.

Resources used?

This web application was made with the Jailbased API and the FBI Wanted API.
